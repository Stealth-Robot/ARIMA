How It Works

A quick guide to getting the most out of A.R.I.M.A.

Rating Navigation Personalisation Pages Editors & Admins Profile

Rating Songs

Click any cell in your column to rate a song 0 to 5. Type a number, then use arrow keys to move to the next song. Notes can be added to any cell (right-click or press N) — cells with notes show a small triangle on hover.

Ratings from other users show up in real time.

Keyboard shortcuts
0–5Set rating (saves instantly, stays active for arrow navigation)
Arrows / WASD / EnterSave and move in any direction
EscapeCancel without saving
Right-click / NAdd or edit a note
Ctrl+Z / Cmd+ZUndo / Redo (add Shift for redo)

Navigation & Discography

  • Click album headers to collapse or expand them. Use Expand All / Collapse All in the discography header. Collapse state is remembered across edit mode toggles and artist navigation.
  • Songs with a Spotify link show a icon. YouTube icons link directly or fall back to a search.
  • Collaborations show as "with Artist" (main) or "feat. Artist" (featured) next to song names.

Pages

  • Artists — Full discography with everyone's ratings side by side. Artist names are colour-coded by gender. Use the bottom bar or hamburger menu to switch.
  • Global Stats — Average scores per artist, colour-coded. Expand to see subunits and soloists.
  • Artist Stats — Rating completion per person. Toggle between "% complete" and "songs remaining". Use "Hide unrated" to filter.
  • Data Approvals — Approve or reject ratings submitted on your behalf. Only shows newly added songs. Filter by For Me, By Me, or All.
  • Changelog & Updates — Searchable log of all edits plus app updates. Filter by user or type.
  • Search — Search icon in the top bar. Type 2+ characters to find artists, albums, or songs.

Personalisation

All settings are on your Profile page and saved between sessions.

  • Themes — Classic, Dark, or a custom theme.
  • Filters — Genre, country, remixes, featured tracks, album sort order.
  • Link visibility — Hide autogenerated YouTube links, all YouTube links, or all Spotify links.
  • Rating key — Customise labels for each score (0–5) and choose which keys to show.
  • Song button size — Adjust edit-mode icon button size (6–30px).

For Editors & Admins

Toggle Edit Mode in the navbar. Everything saves instantly.

Inline editing
  • Artist name, gender, country — Click to edit.
  • Album name, genres, type, release date — Click any in the album header.
  • Song name — Click to rename.
  • Remix / Promoted — Checkboxes in the extra columns.
  • Spotify & YouTube URLs — Click the greyed-out icons on a song row.
  • Rate for other users — Click any user's rating cell.

♪Manage artists (main / featured)

Album controls

Duplicates
  • Songs in multiple albums are tagged duplicate (hidden outside edit mode).
  • Click the tag to override which album is primary.
  • Use ✂ to split into an independent copy with all ratings cloned.
Adding content
  • Add Album — Green button at the bottom of the artist page.
  • Add song to album — + icon in the album header.
  • Add Artist — From the navbar. Tooltip on submit shows missing fields.
  • Spotify import — Paste a Spotify URL to auto-fill albums, tracks, and links.
Subunits & Soloists
  • Convert — Buttons at the bottom of an artist page to link under a parent (password required).
  • Unlink — 🔗 next to a subunit/soloist header to make them standalone again.
Deleting
  • Song — Red ✕ next to the name (password required).
  • Album — Red ✕ left of the header (shows impact count, password required).
  • Artist — Red button at the bottom of the page (password required).
  • Changelog entry — Admins only, small ✕ on each entry.

Profile & Account

  • Profile image — Set a custom image URL.
  • Reset password — Change your password anytime.
  • Settings — Theme, filters, album sort, button size, rating key labels, and link visibility.
