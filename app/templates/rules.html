How It Works

A quick guide to getting the most out of A.R.I.M.A.

Rating Songs

Click any cell in your column to rate a song from 0 to 5. The colour-coded key on the artist page shows what each score means. You can also leave notes on any cell — even unrated ones. Cells with notes have a small triangle in the corner; hover to read them.

Ratings from other users show up in real time — no need to refresh.

Keyboard Shortcuts

You can rate quickly without touching the mouse once you've clicked into a cell.

Key What it does
0–5Set the rating
EnterSave and move to the next song
Arrow keys / WASDSave and move in any direction
EscapeCancel without saving
Right-click / NAdd or edit a note
Ctrl+Z / Cmd+ZUndo last rating change
Ctrl+Shift+Z / Cmd+Shift+ZRedo last rating change

Finding Your Way Around

  • Artists — The main page. Pick an artist from the bottom bar and see their full discography with everyone's ratings side by side. Artist names are colour-coded by gender (pink / blue / green). On smaller screens, tap the hamburger menu to find an artist.
  • Global Stats — See average scores per artist, colour-coded so you can spot who rates what at a glance. Expand an artist to see subunits and soloists.
  • Artist Stats — Check how much each person has rated. Switch between "% complete" and "songs remaining" with the dropdown.
  • Submissions — New artists and albums waiting for approval show up here.
  • Changelog — A searchable log of everything that's been added.
  • Search — Hit the search icon in the top bar and type at least 2 characters. Click a result to jump straight to it.

Personalisation

  • Filters — Use the genre and country dropdowns to narrow what you see. You can also toggle remixes and featured tracks on or off, and sort albums newest- or oldest-first.
  • Themes — Switch between Classic, Dark, or a custom theme on your profile page.
  • All your settings are saved automatically and stick between sessions.

For Editors & Admins

Toggle Edit Mode in the navbar to unlock all editing tools on the artist page. Everything saves instantly — no separate save button needed.

What you can edit inline

  • Album name, release date & genres — Click any of these in the album header to edit. Genres open a checkbox picker.
  • Song name — Click a song name to rename it.
  • Remix / Promoted — Tick the checkboxes in the extra columns that appear in edit mode.
  • Artist country — Click the country label next to the artist name to change it.

Moving & organising

  • Move a song — The small ⇵ icon next to each song lets you move it to a different album.

Adding content

  • Add Album — The green button at the bottom of the page. Set a name, type, date, genres, and songs. You can add collaborating artists to each song. Editor submissions are auto-approved.
  • Add Artist — Available from the navbar in edit mode. Create a new artist with albums, songs, genres, and collaborations.
  • Collaborations — Songs with multiple main artists automatically show "feat." labels.

Deleting

  • Delete a song or album — The small red ✕ next to songs and album headers. You'll need to enter your password to confirm.
  • Delete an artist — The red button at the bottom of the page. Removes the artist and everything under them. Password required.
